When we work together
@pepitoperez

Nothing fuels a gear head’s heart more than a good, old-fashioned restoration story. There’s something magical and undeniably cool about finding an old car and devoting your time to it until it’s shinier than the day it rolled off the assembly line. The dedication, the frustration and the unadulterated passion for such projects is what makes them so interesting, but classic cars create lasting relationships between the car and its driver as well as in the tight-knit restoration community, which is the real reason classic cars are so irresistible.

My safari story
@sultanito

It all started when we were holidaying in Kenya and dad decided to make our holiday better we should go on an African safari. So we packed our things and went on an adventurous safari. We hired a tour guide and he drove us to our camp site which took at least 5 hours. I slept half of the way. We knew we were getting close as we saw herds of zebras grazing. It was my first sight of animals in the wild. It was the most amazing thing I had ever seen. When we arrived at our campsite we saw strange looking men with red material wrapped around their bodies, jumping up and down as high as any kangaroo and they had huge earlobes with strange beaded earrings in their ears.
